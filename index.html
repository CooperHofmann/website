About the studio

Cooper of the Crop is a visual atelier led by photographer Cooper Hale. With a documentary approach and an eye for nostalgic tones, the studio partners with growers, artisanal brands, and sustainable hospitality teams to craft imagery that feels handwritten and honest.

Each project begins with listening: to your story, your community, and the seasonal cadence that makes your brand unique. The result is a gallery of photographs that feel grounded, intentional, and effortless.
